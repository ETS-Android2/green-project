Green & Fresh Project

The purpose of this project is introduce the "Internet of things" in the Industry. Fruits have different characteristics that determine if a fruit is fresh or not to consume like color, humidity, PH, smell and other kind of things, so, the project seeks to start analyzing the color of the fruits to verify their status and will progress in a future with different features.
